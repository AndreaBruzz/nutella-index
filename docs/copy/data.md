## 📊 Data Information & Disclaimer

### Where does the data come from?

The dataset behind this project started from an unconventional (and fun) source:  
**YouTube videos from HumanSafari (Nicolò Balini)**.

We analyzed video transcripts to extract mentions of Nutella prices around the world. This process involved a mix of automated techniques and manual validation to turn casual mentions into structured data.

Over time, the dataset has expanded beyond that initial source.

---

### 🤝 Community contributions

Some entries may have been **added by users** who wanted to contribute prices from their own countries or travels.

To maintain a reasonable level of quality:

- All user-submitted data is **reviewed and approved by an admin** before being published
- We try to ensure consistency in formatting and completeness of information

---

### ⚠️ Accuracy disclaimer

While we do our best to keep the data clean and reliable, this project is ultimately based on:

- manually mentioned prices in videos
- user-contributed entries
- currency conversions and normalization

Because of this:

- Prices may be **approximate or outdated**
- Some entries may contain **minor inaccuracies or inconsistencies**
- Availability, promotions, and local factors can significantly affect real-world prices
