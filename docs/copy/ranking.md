## 🟤 Global Nutella Ranking

**Where is Nutella the most expensive in the world? And where can you get it for the best price?**

This global ranking compares the price of Nutella across countries using a standardized metric: **EUR per 100 grams (EUR/100g)**. By normalizing prices this way, we make it easy to compare jars of different sizes and currencies on a like-for-like basis.

Whether you're a traveler, a curious foodie, or just someone who loves Nutella, this ranking gives you a unique snapshot of how prices vary around the world.

---

## 💡 How to read this ranking

Each country is ranked based on the **average price of Nutella per 100g in euros**.

- **EUR/100g**: This is the key metric. It converts local prices into euros and adjusts for package size, so everything is directly comparable.
- **Samples**: The number of price observations collected for that country. More samples generally mean a more reliable average.
- **Latest update**: Shows how recent the data is, so you can judge how fresh the price information is.

Inside each country, you’ll find detailed entries showing:
- Product size (e.g. 200g, 750g)
- Local price and currency
- Calculated EUR/100g value
- Date of collection
- Source of the data

---

## 🌍 What this ranking shows

Nutella prices can vary **dramatically** from country to country. Factors like import costs, local demand, taxes, and distribution all play a role.

- Some countries rank at the top with **premium prices**, where Nutella is considered more of a luxury item.
- Others offer it at surprisingly **affordable prices**, often due to local availability or lower costs of goods.
